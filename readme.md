AntifragiCity Workpackage 4

- The codebase and deliverables related to WP4
- T4.1 Antifragile model for urban mobility: 
  - Simulations based on the [opensource SUMO scenario for Luxembourg City](https://sumo.dlr.de/docs/networks/luxembourg.html)
  - KPIs based on AntifragiCity Task 2.7 are incorporated in the simulation environment
  - KPIs across six domains: 
    - network and system performance, 
    - transport infrastructure, 
    - travel characteristics, 
    - transport capacity and resilience, 
    - ecological impacts, and
    - energy consumption
